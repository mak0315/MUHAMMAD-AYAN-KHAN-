01 — About

A Story of Many Passions

Hello I'm Muhammad Ayan Khan, a multidisciplinary creator based in Abbottabad, Pakistan. My journey spans continents and crafts from serving as a Former Security Officer at Golden Eagle Security in Qatar, to the literary world as a published author, to the lens of a photographer with over 15,000 Instagram followers.

I was a Club Cricket Player and the founder of Havelian Kings sport shaped my discipline and leadership. Today I channel that drive into building two startups (Kor Da & Pactum), managing Airbnb properties in Islamabad, freelancing globally, and creating content that moves people.
